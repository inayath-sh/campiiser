## Guidelines for the Reading Material

The following readings are recommended for participants who would like a basic introduction to the topics covered in CAMP 2026. You need not read everything; consult them only for topics that are unfamiliar or if you would like a refresher.

## Preparatory Material for CAMP 2026

### Functions, Limits, Vector Algebra, and Basic Complex Analysis

- We expect a very basic understanding of these topics. If you need to brush up, please go through [**Viktor Jirsa's Mathbootcamp**](https://mpcrlab.com/uploads/documents/Mathematics-Boot-Camp.pdf) (also provided on the shared drive).
- Complex analysis: YouTube lectures by [**Prof. Samudra Roy (IIT Kharagpur)**.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9MTqD7yxHWg&list=PLq-Gm0yRYwThklRVGuMC01Gl7m1YSv_qn&index=1&pp=iAQB)

### Calculus

- Basic calculus intuition: **3Blue1Brown – Essence of Calculus**.
- Easy and intuitive introduction to calculus by Khan Academy:
  - [Calculus 1](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/calculus-1)
  - [Calculus 2](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/calculus-2)
- For a basic introduction to calculus: [**Viktor Jirsa's Mathbootcamp**](https://mpcrlab.com/uploads/documents/Mathematics-Boot-Camp.pdf)
### Differential Equations

- [MIT OpenCourseWare: Differential Equations](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-03-differential-equations-spring-2010/video_galleries/video-lectures/).
- Advanced Engineering Mathematics (H. K. Dass)
  - Chapter 3: Differential Equations
  - Chapter 15: Numerical Techniques
- Mathematical Methods in the Physical Sciences — Mary L. Boas.

### Nonlinear Dynamics

- Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos — Steven H. Strogatz.
- Dynamics: The Geometry of Behavior — Ralph Abraham & Christopher D. Shaw.
- [Lectures by Steven Strogatz](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ycJEoqmQvwg&list=PLbN57C5Zdl6j_qJA-pARJnKsmROzPnO9V).
- Concise lectures and overview by [NLD lectures by Faculty of Khan](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLdgVBOaXkb9C8iPDD5xW0jT-c3dtP4TR5&feature=shared).
- Notes by Dimitri Kartofelev [Link](https://www.tud.ttu.ee/web/dmitri.kartofelev/YFX1560/LectureNotes_1.pdf) (uploaded on the shared drive).
- Chaos and Integrability in Nonlinear Dynamics: An Introduction — Michael Tabor.

### Computational Neuroscience

- MIT 9.40: Introduction to Neural Computation [Playlist link](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLUl4u3cNGP61I4aI5T6OaFfRK2gihjiMm).
- Coursera course on computational neuroscience by Rajesh Rao and Adrienne Fairhall:
  https://www.coursera.org/course/compneuro
- Basic understanding of the Hodgkin–Huxley neuron model [Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=no_1cElnSIQ).
- Books:
  - Theoretical Neuroscience — Dayan & Abbott
  - Dynamical Systems in Neuroscience — Eugene M. Izhikevich
  - Methods in Neuronal Modeling (2nd Edition) — Koch & Segev
- Neuronal Dynamics Online:
  https://neuronaldynamics.epfl.ch/online/index.html
- Basic neuroscience:
  - Neuroscience — Dale Purves
  - Foundations of Cellular Neurophysiology — Johnston & Wu

### Probability and Statistics

- The Cartoon Guide to Statistics — Larry Gonick & Woollcott Smith.
- Khan Academy: Statistics and Probability.
- An Introduction to Probability Theory and Its Applications — William Feller.
- A First Course in Probability — Sheldon Ross.

  https://lectures.scientific-python.org/

### Books for Scripting in NEURON and XPPAUT (Optional)

- The NEURON Book.
- A Guide to XPPAUT for Students and Researchers.
- Both are available on the shared drive.
